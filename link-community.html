Schools & Clubs

LINK Lake Norman™ partners with schools, homeschool pods, youth groups, sports teams, and civic organizations across the region. Get your students connected to real career pathways.

Partner With Future Ready™

🏫
Explore It™ — Middle School (7th & 8th)

Immersive career exploration for 7th & 8th graders. Students build life skills, discover strengths, and chart a course before high school. Available for schools, pods & youth orgs.

  • Resilience, accountability & self-discovery
  • Financial literacy & money management
  • Professional etiquette & communication
  • Course path planning aligned to goals

Invest In It™ — High School (Ages 16–22)

Career-connected learning with real internship placement. Students explore careers hands-on, gain mentorship, and build skills that open doors to trade school, college, or direct employment.

  • Real internship placement with vetted employers
  • Resume writing, interview prep & career coaching
  • Pathway exploration: trade, 2-yr, 4-yr, cert, or work
  • Bilingual students — your value rises significantly
